Can someone explain ARP Spoofing vs DNS Poisoning? 🤔 I keep confusing them in exams.
A
Admin 9:22 AM ✓ Verified
ARP Spoofing = Layer 2 (MAC spoofing, local network). DNS Poisoning = Layer 7 (domain names). Both are MITM but at different OSI layers! 🎯
